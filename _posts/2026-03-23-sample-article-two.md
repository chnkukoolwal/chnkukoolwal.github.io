title: Why Every Founder Should Track Metrics Weekly
date: 2026-03-23 09:00:00
---
Metrics are not just numbers; they are signals. Founders who track their key performance indicators weekly can:

- Adjust product roadmap proactively
- Identify growth opportunities earlier
- Reduce cash burn through informed decisions

Even simple dashboards help. Weekly reflection drives better decision-making and aligns the team toward measurable outcomes.
