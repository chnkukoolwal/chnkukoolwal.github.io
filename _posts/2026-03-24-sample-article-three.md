Being a startup advisor is about influence and trust. To build a professional network:

- Engage with founders on a weekly basis
- Share insights publicly (articles, social media)
- Attend sector-specific events and meetups

A focused and consistent presence builds credibility. Quality over quantity always applies when establishing relationships that lead to impactful partnerships.
